IIT Climbing FAQ




This section contains a wealth of information, related to IIT Climbing. If you cannot find an answer to your question, make sure to contact us. Also, if you see any errors please let us know as well.

General questions

How do I sign up for the IIT Climbing Club and get on the mailing list?

To sign up please fill out this form. This will also get you on our mailing list.

I have signed up for the mailing list, now what?

We send out an weekly email with the climbing activities for the given week. If you want to join any of the trips, please reply to our email saying so. It's first come first serve. Also, climbers of any skill level are welcome!
We do have specialty events such as outdoor events, beginner trips, and clinics which are prioritized for that given event.

Isn't climbing dangerous?

Yes it is, but with advancements in climbing technologies the sport is relatively safe. Our motto is that "safety is cool". This means as long as you follow safety guideline there is minimal chance of getting injured.

What essential gear do I need to climb?

Besides a good attitude and keeping safety in mind, you don't need anything. The club will lend out harness to climbers but we do suggest purchasing your own. Please wear adequate clothing that will not limit your movements. Most gyms have a bathroom/fitting room were you can change. Also, please bring water and a snack. However, most gyms do have drinking fountains. Check Beginner Gear buying section.

What gear should I buy as a Beginner?

There are three main things we would suggest. Harness, shoes and belay device with locking biner. For harness, go to a outdoors store (REI, Moosejaw, or Climbing gym) and try it on will be the best suggestion and they go for ~$40. As for shoes, La Sportiva Tarantulace Climbing Shoe is a great for the money, usually goes for around ~$75. Lastly for the belay device and locking biner, any reputable climbing company sell them together for around ~$25 .
So your looking at ~$140 total and if you take care of your gear this will last years.

Black Diamond offers a beginners package for cheap. If the package is available, it will bring down the cost even more.

What gear do I need for _______ Gym?

For all gyms, please bring adequate clothing for getting there and for climbing. Most gyms have a bathroom/locker room where you could change. Also, please bring water and a snack. Most gyms do have drinking fountains but you never know.

Bouldering (Hidden Peak) - Just bring climbing shoes
Bouldering/Rope (Vertical Endeavors, ClimbOn, Brooklyn Boulders, First Ascent) - Harness, Shoes, and Belay device

*If you don't own this gear please let the club know.

Club gear doesn't fit me or there already spoken for, what are my options?

You can rent from the climbing gym (about ~$5-15 depending on what your getting and at what gym. Or, you could buy your own gear. Check out "What gear should I Buy" Section.

When will I get reimbursed for driving?

We will try to get you the money right after the trip. There maybe some delayed paper work so please be patient and remind us if you haven't received anything or got updated within two weeks after the trip.

Where do we climb outdoors?

Pretty much everywhere in the US!
